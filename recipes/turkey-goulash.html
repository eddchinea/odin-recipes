Ingredients for Turkey Goulash

  • 1.5 pounds (680g) ground turkey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sweet paprika
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups (475ml) chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds (optional, for a traditional touch)
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
